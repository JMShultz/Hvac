Most common AC problems?

Most common air conditioning issues can include unit not cooling properly, and frequent on-and-off cycling. This can be caused by refrigerant leaks, clogged filters, or malfunctioning thermostats.

What causes AC units to freeze up?

There are a lot of reasons why your air conditioning system might freeze up. Some cases low refrigerant can cause your system to freeze up. Over time, wear and tear to your system can result in leaks. Dirty evaporator coils can cause units to lose airflow and freeze up.
